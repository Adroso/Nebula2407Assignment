Road Safety

The annual economic cost of road crashes in Australia is enormous—estimated at $27 billion per annum—and the social impacts are devastating.So we decided to develop solutions and campaigns to keep drivers, cyclists, pedestrians and motorcyclists informed of the best and latest road safety measures. Choosing safer vehicles could help save your life in you are in a crash.

Since record keeping commenced in 1925, there have been over 187,000 deaths on Australia's roads. However, road trauma levels have declined substantially over the last four decades, despite considerable population growth and a threefold increase in registered motor vehicles. During this period, the number of road deaths per year has fallen from 3,798 deaths in 1970 to 1,206 in 2015.

Nebula Developers have now created a set of visualisations to help people understand the impact of road safety.
